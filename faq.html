How the game works?

The game generates a number between one and one hundred randomly every game and you bet that the number will be between a range.

For example if you bet that the result will be between 1 to 49 that mean you have a 48% of winning and your payout is two times your bet.

As you can see there is 1% missing given that the house edge is 1%, that is used to make a simultaneous bet to win the jackpot of 1BTC, the probability for you to win the bitcoin is: (Your wager/100)/1BTC.
This means that if you bet 1BTC you have one in a hundred ods to win the jackpot.

If you bet 100BTC you are warranted to get the 1BTC jackpot.

If you bet more than 100BTC you are warranted to get 1% of your bet back to ensure the 1% house edge for big bets.

For the sake of simplicity the only two parameters that you can edit of your bet are the wager and the probability from 1% to 97% in integer units, this let us do a simple and fun interface to bet.
