How does the game works?

The game generates a number between 1 and 100, and allows you to bet on what you think the result will be.

Your probability of winning, and payout for guessing correctly is proportional to how specific the range you bet. The payout of guessing correct is such that the house edge would be 2%

However, the house edge is 1%, not 2%. This is because we use the 1% as a chance to win the jackpot. For instance if the jackpot is 1 BTC, your probability of winning it is: wager/100/(wager + 1 BTC)
You can always see your chance of winning the jackpot while in the game.
